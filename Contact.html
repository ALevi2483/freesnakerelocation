Need Immediate Snake Help?

Do not email for time-sensitive snake relocation requests.

If you need a snake relocated, please visit our Find a Volunteer page. You can search in three ways:

  • Find the Closest Volunteer: Enter your address to locate the three nearest volunteers.
  • Search by State and City: Use dropdown menus to filter by state and city.
  • View the Volunteer Map: Use our interactive map to zoom in on your location and contact someone nearby.

Once you locate a volunteer:

  • Send them a text message. Most volunteers respond faster to text than calls from unknown numbers.

Note: Free Snake Relocation does not provide emergency dispatch services. All volunteers operate independently and are not supervised, endorsed, or dispatched by this directory.

Volunteer Listing Updates or Removal

Need to update or remove your listing from the directory? You can either:

  • Submit a new form and select: “I'm already listed but need to update my information”.
  • Email us directly with your name, city, and the requested update at: FreeSnakeRelocation@gmail.com
