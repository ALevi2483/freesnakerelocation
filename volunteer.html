Join as a Volunteer - Free Snake Relocation Directory

📢 Why Volunteer?

The Free Snake Relocation Directory connects volunteer snake handlers with people in need of safe, humane snake removals. By joining as a volunteer, you help protect both people and snakes while promoting conservation and education in your community.

🤝 Who Can Volunteer?

  • Experienced snake handlers who understand local species
  • Wildlife professionals or herpetology enthusiasts
  • Individuals willing to comply with local wildlife laws
  • Those with access to proper tools (snake hooks, tongs, secure transport containers)

✅ Before You Submit

  • ✅ You meet your state or local permit requirements
  • ✅ You agree to be listed publicly
  • ✅ You understand this is a volunteer listing, not a dispatch service

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a permit to relocate snakes?

Some states require permits for handling or relocating wildlife. Check your state’s regulations before signing up.

Can I charge for my services?

No, all relocations must be free of charge, but you may accept voluntary donations for travel expenses.

What if I am unavailable when someone calls?

If you are unable to respond, we encourage people to check the directory for another available relocator nearby.

How can I improve my snake handling skills?

We recommend reaching out to local herpetological societies, wildlife rescue groups, or experienced relocators for guidance and training.
