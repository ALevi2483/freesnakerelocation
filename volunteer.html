Volunteer for Free Snake Removal and Relocation

📢 Why Volunteer?

The Free Snake Relocation Directory connects volunteer snake handlers with people in need of safe, humane snake removals. By joining as a volunteer, you help protect both people and snakes while promoting conservation and education in your community.

You are solely responsible for your own actions, decisions, communications, safety practices, legal compliance, permits, equipment, transportation, and conduct.

Before submitting the form, read the full volunteer disclaimer and acknowledgment below. You can also review the full site disclaimer here.

🦎 Who Can Volunteer?

  • Experienced snake handlers who understand local species.
  • Wildlife professionals or herpetology enthusiasts.
  • Individuals willing to comply with local wildlife laws.
  • Those with proper tools such as hooks, tongs, and containers.

❓ FAQ

Do I need a permit to relocate snakes?

Some states require permits for handling or relocating wildlife. Check your local regulations before signing up.

Can I charge for my services?

No. All relocations must be free of charge. You may accept voluntary donations for travel costs if that is lawful in your area and consistent with your listing rules.

What if I am unavailable when someone calls?

Users may check the directory for another available relocator nearby. You are not required to respond to every request.

How can I improve my snake handling skills?

We recommend reaching out to local herpetological societies, rescue groups, wildlife professionals, or experienced relocators in your area.
