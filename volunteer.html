Join as a Volunteer - Free Snake Relocation Directory

Why Volunteer?

The Free Snake Relocation Directory connects volunteer snake handlers with people in need of safe, humane snake removals. By joining as a volunteer, you help protect both people and snakes while promoting conservation and education in your community.

Who Can Volunteer?

  • Experienced snake handlers who understand local species.
  • Wildlife professionals or herpetology enthusiasts.
  • Individuals willing to comply with local wildlife laws.
  • Those with access to proper tools (snake hooks, tongs, secure transport containers).

What Happens After You Sign Up?

  • Your information will be added to our interactive map for public reference.
  • You may be contacted directly by people in need of a relocation.
  • You can update or remove your listing at any time by contacting us.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need a permit to relocate snakes?
A: Some states require permits for handling or relocating wildlife. Check your state’s regulations before signing up.

Q: Can I charge for my services?
A: No, all relocations must be free of charge, but you may accept voluntary donations for travel expenses.

Q: What if I am unavailable when someone calls?
A: If you are unable to respond, we encourage people to check the directory for another available relocator nearby.

Q: How can I improve my snake handling skills?
A: We recommend reaching out to local herpetological societies, wildlife rescue groups, or experienced relocators for guidance and training.
