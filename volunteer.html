Do I need a permit to relocate snakes?

Some states require permits for handling or relocating wildlife. Check your local regulations before signing up.

Can I charge for my services?

No. All relocations must be free of charge. You may accept voluntary donations for travel costs.

What if I am unavailable when someone calls?

We encourage users to check the directory for another available relocator nearby.

How can I improve my snake handling skills?

We recommend reaching out to local herpetological societies, rescue groups, or other relocators.
