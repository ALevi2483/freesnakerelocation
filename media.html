Need Immediate Snake Help?

Do not email for time-sensitive snake relocation requests.

If you need a snake relocated, please use one of the following methods:

  • Click on Closest Relocator and enter your address to find the three nearest volunteers.
  • Use the Search by State and City Tool to find a relocator by city and state.
  • Or, click on the Relocator Map, zoom into your area, and click on a nearby relocator.
Once you locate a relocator:
  • Send them a text message. Not everyone answers calls from unfamiliar numbers. Texting is the best way to get a fast response.

Note: We do not provide emergency dispatch services. All relocators operate independently.
