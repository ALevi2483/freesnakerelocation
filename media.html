Media & Press Resources

FreeSnakeRelocation.com is a nationwide, volunteer-powered directory helping homeowners, businesses, and communities safely and humanely remove snakes—free of charge. All individuals listed are independent volunteers and are not employed, assigned, or dispatched by the site or its administrators.

Fast Facts

  • Volunteers in: 43+ states and expanding to U.S. territories and Canada
  • Cost to public: $0 — always free
  • Mission: Education, conservation, and coexistence with native wildlife

What Makes This Newsworthy

  • Run entirely by unpaid volunteers with real-world snake handling experience
  • Offers an alternative to pest control or police for snake-related calls
  • Focused on preventing unnecessary harm to native species during peak snake season
  • Founded by everyday citizens passionate about wildlife and public education
