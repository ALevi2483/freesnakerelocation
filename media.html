Media & Press Resources

FreeSnakeRelocation.com is a nationwide volunteer network helping homeowners, businesses, and communities safely and humanely remove snakes—free of charge.

Fast Facts

  • Volunteers in: 30+ states and growing
  • Cost to public: $0 — always free
  • Mission: Education, conservation, coexistence

What Makes This Newsworthy

  • Run entirely by unpaid volunteers with real-world snake handling experience
  • Responds to emergency calls that would otherwise go to pest control or police
  • Built to prevent unnecessary harm to native snakes, especially during peak warm-weather seasons
  • Founded by individuals passionate about wildlife and public education
