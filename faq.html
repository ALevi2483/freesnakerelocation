What is the Free Snake Relocation Directory?

A volunteer-based network that connects individuals with local volunteers who relocate snakes free of charge.

How do I find a snake relocator near me?

Use our interactive map or the "Closest Relocator" tool to find a relocator near you. If no one is available, check back later or consult local wildlife control.

Will I be charged?

No. Volunteers agree to relocate snakes for free. Some may offer separate services, but relocations listed through this directory are free.

What areas does the directory cover?

We have volunteers across many U.S. states. Use the interactive map to explore available relocators near you.

Use our Relocator Map or Closest Relocator tool to contact a nearby volunteer directly. Once you find a relocator, send them a text message ‚Äî texting is the fastest way to reach someone.
