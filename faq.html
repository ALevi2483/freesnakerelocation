Do I need to submit a new form to change my data?

You can either submit a new form and select "I'm already listed, but need to update my information" or just text the Wizard at 603-921-0994.

What is the Free Snake Relocation Directory?

A volunteer-based network that connects individuals with local volunteers who relocate snakes free of charge.

How do I find a snake relocator near me?

Use our interactive map to find a relocator near you. If no one is available, check back later or consult local wildlife control.

Will I be charged?

No. Volunteers agree to relocate snakes for free. Some may offer separate services, but may not charge for relocations through this directory.

What areas does the directory cover?

We have volunteers across many U.S. states. Use the interactive map to explore.

How do I request help?

Use our Relocator Map or Closest Relocator tool on the site to contact a nearby volunteer directly.

What information should I provide?

Location, description of the snake if possible, where it's located (yard, attic, garage, etc.), and any relevant safety info.
