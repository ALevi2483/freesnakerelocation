Snakebite Support & Identification Tools

National Snakebite Support is a nonprofit organization that connects snakebite victims, pet owners, and healthcare providers with evidence-based guidance. Their expert-moderated Facebook group provides real-time support and education.

Crofab is a trusted site offering treatment information and clinical resources for venomous snakebites in the United States. It includes regional species insights and antivenom guidance for healthcare professionals.

Snake Snap is a mobile app and website that helps users identify snakes by uploading photos. It provides state-specific species databases and interactive identification tools for the public.

Conservation Organizations

Save the Snakes is a global nonprofit focused on snake conservation and reducing human-snake conflict through education, advocacy, and direct outreach.

The Rattlesnake Conservancy works to preserve rattlesnake species through conservation programs, habitat protection, public education, and professional training.

The Reptile Database is a scientific database offering up-to-date taxonomic and natural history data on all living reptile species, including snakes, lizards, turtles, and more.
