Snakebite, Identification, and Conservation Resources

Resources

National Snakebite Support is a registered nonprofit organization dedicated to connecting snakebite victims, pet owners, and healthcare providers with experts who practice and promote evidence-based snakebite management. You can also connect to their Facebook page by clicking here.

Crofab Website is a site where you can find out more information about the venomous snakes in your state.

Snake Snap is an interactive platform that provides a comprehensive database of snake species in the U.S., organized by state. Users can upload photos for real-time identification and access quick reference field guides.

Save the Snakes is a nonprofit organization dedicated exclusively to snake conservation and human-snake conflict mitigation.

The Rattlesnake Conservancy is a nonprofit organization committed to the long-term preservation of rattlesnake species.

The Reptile Database is a scientific database providing taxonomic and natural history information on all living reptile species (including snakes, lizards, turtles, etc.). Visit: The Reptile Database
