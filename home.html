What is Free Snake Relocation?

We are a volunteer-powered, national directory that connects people with snake relocation volunteers across the U.S. Our goal is to prevent unnecessary killing of snakes by connecting the public with people who can safely and humanely remove and relocate them.

How Does It Work?

Volunteers add their information to our public map. You can search by state, city, or GPS to find help near you. We don't charge any fees and volunteers are not compensated by us ‚Äì any arrangements must be made directly between you and the volunteer.
